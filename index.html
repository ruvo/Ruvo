Ruvo



Artist Information

Ruvo is an electronica artist based in Denver, Colorado. Born in York U.K. Joshua Crowley established himself as a musician at an early age. First teaching himself the guitar from the age of 16, he then broadened his musical repertoire to playing piano, drums and vocals.


After studying performing music in York, U.K. Joshua Crowley began to record and produce his own music using Ableton Live. He then went on to have two of his songs played on BBC Introducing in 2012. After playing at various venues in the U.K and the U.S. and receiving some positive feedback Joshua Crowley's musical tastes adapted to a more electronic style creating rich textures and evolving sounds. In June 2015 he started producing under the name Ruvo and is now working towards his first E.P. Sirens. The forthcoming album is scheduled for release in 2017.
