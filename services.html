Services


We aim to please our customers by offering a first time free estimate!
Step One - Call us at our office in Denver to outline details of what services you require and we can get the ball rolling right away


Step Two - We will arrange a convenient time to come to your home and give you a free estimate. We can also work with your measurements and may be able to give you a quote over the phone


Step Three - We will then book you into our calendar and arrange a suitable time to install your new carpet. We offer a lifetime guarantee and will provide you with a contract breaking down the costs included in the installation


Step Four - Congratulations your new carpet is ready to be installed. Don't worry, we will maintain communication throughtout the whole process and provide you with the appropriate aftercare information to ensure your carpet stays in pristine condition


Padding

Properly selected padding will sit underneath the carpet and allow it to lay flat. Both your carpet and padding need to go together. Good carpet padding will be pretty firm and not completely compress when you give it a squeeze. Lower quality padding will almost completely smash down and this will create extra wear on your new carpet.
